Get ready to Roll, Jump, Spin and Bump!

Ball Balance is a journey through a mysterious Stone Tower, where you get the chance to explore numerous levels filled with various mechanisms. Assume direct control over the Ball and transform into 3 different forms - each with unique properties. Use physics to your advantage, but try to think things over before you act - destroying everything will not necessarily lead you to an exit!

Features:

  • Physics based puzzle - have some fun with pushing things around!
  • Tranfsorm into 3 different forms - Stone, Wood or Paper - each with its own pros and cons.
  • Puzzle mechanisms - draw bridges, platforms, fans and more.
  • 30 unique levels.

Challenge the Stone Tower's Constructor and solve the riddles that he left for you on every floor of the tower!
